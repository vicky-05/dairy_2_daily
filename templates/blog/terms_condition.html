Terms & Conditions

1. General Terms

Dairy to Daily provides fresh dairy products directly from farms, ensuring high quality and hygiene. Our products are perishable, and we follow strict safety standards.

2. Order & Delivery

Orders must be placed through our official channels. Deliveries occur twice daily within a 50 km radius. Customers must provide accurate address details for successful delivery.

3. Payment & Pricing

Prices are subject to change. Payments must be made online or via approved methods. Cash on Delivery may be available in selected areas.

4. Returns & Refunds

Due to the perishable nature of our products, returns are not accepted. If the product is damaged, customers must report it within 2 hours with proof.

5. Subscription Plans

Subscription services allow daily dairy deliveries. Customers can modify plans with 24-hour notice before the next delivery.

6. Privacy Policy

Customer data is confidential and used only for order processing. We do not share information with third parties without consent.

For any queries, contact us at support@dairytodaily.com
