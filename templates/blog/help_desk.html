Help & Support

1. How to Use the DToD Chatbot

The Dairy to Daily chatbot is your personal assistant for quick help. Simply type your query in the chatbox, and the bot will provide instant answers regarding orders, subscriptions, and payments.

2. How to Make Subscriptions & Payments

To subscribe, visit the 'Subscription Plans' section and choose your preferred plan. Payments can be made via UPI, credit/debit card, or wallet.

4. How to Change Your Profile Image

Go to 'My Profile,' click on the profile picture, and upload a new image. Supported formats: JPG, PNG.

5. How to Track Your Order

You can track your order by visiting the 'My Orders' section and clicking on 'Track Order.'

6. Delivery Timings & Areas

Deliveries are made twice daily: Morning (6 AM - 9 AM) & Evening (5 PM - 8 PM). We deliver within a 50 km radius of the city.

7. Refund & Cancellation Policy

If your order is incorrect or damaged, request a refund within 24 hours. Cancellations must be made before the cut-off time (10 PM for next-day delivery).

8. How to Contact Customer Support

Need help? Our support team is available from 8 AM - 10 PM. Email: support@dairytodaily.com Phone: +91 98765 43210
