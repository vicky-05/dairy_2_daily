About Us

About Dairy to Daily

Dairy to Daily (DToD) is a dedicated initiative to bring fresh, organic milk and its by-products straight from our farm to your home. With our city-based farm, we aim to connect urban families with natural, high-quality dairy products.

Our mission is to ensure that every household in the city gets fresh, nutritious milk and other dairy products every day. We believe in sustainability, quality, and customer satisfaction.

Vision

To ensure every household in our city has access to 100% natural and fresh dairy products daily.

Mission

To maintain the highest standards in dairy farming and distribution while fostering a sustainable and healthy environment.

Our Impact

150+ Cows

1000+ Deliveries

What is DToD?

At Dairy to Daily, we specialize in producing and delivering fresh cow milk and its by-products such as curd, cheese, and palkova. Our farm operates within a 50 km radius, ensuring freshness and quality in every product.
